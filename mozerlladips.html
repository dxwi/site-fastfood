Mozzarella Dippers

The Mozzarella Dippers were replaced with the Cheese Melt Dippers.

The Mozzarella Dippers were discontinued due to a lawsuit made by a man in California against McDonalds for claiming
that the cheese contains more than 3% starch. McDonalds claimed there was 100% mozzarella in the cheese.

When brought to the Official McDonald'sMozzarella Dippers page, you would not find
any information regarding the nutriontal values, or allergen information.
